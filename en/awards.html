Jury Award

In March 2009, the Chord Scale Generator was awarded with the Eclipse Community Award (Category: "Best Commercial Rich Client Platform Application") at the international developer conference EclipseCon in Santa Clara (California, USA). A panel of judges consisting of journalists and developers evaluated applications on their user interfaces, innovation and comprehensiveness.
