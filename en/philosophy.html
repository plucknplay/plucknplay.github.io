Philosophy

The Chord Scale Generator follows a simple but good idea.

It doesn't matter,

  • what string instrument you are playing (guitar, bass, ukulele, banjo ...),
  • what kind of music you are playing (rock, jazz, hardcore-punk, classical ...),
  • whether you are a beginner or a professional,
  • which notations you are familiar with (musical notation, tablature, boxes ...) or if you know any notation at all
  • whether you are left- or right-hander,
  • whether you use the operating system Windows, Mac OS or Linux.

We hope the Chord Scale Generator will help you to improve your playing. Perhaps you will even gain new knowledge of your instrument.
